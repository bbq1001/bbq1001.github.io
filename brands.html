Our brand identity and trademark information for MyGymBro.

What is a Trademark?

A trademark (Marke) is a legally protected sign that distinguishes the products or services of one company from those of others. Trademarks can take various forms including words, images, colors, sounds, and more.

MyGymBro utilizes three distinct trademark forms to establish and protect our brand identity in the health and wellness software market.

Our Trademarks

Word Mark

MyGymBro

Our word mark "MyGymBro" represents our brand name in text form. It combines "My" (personal), "Gym" (fitness), and "Bro" (friendly companion).

Usage: Website, app interface, marketing materials, documentation, and all official communications.
